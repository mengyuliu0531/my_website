Who am I?

My name is Mengyu LIU and I am from China (Mengyu means wings of dreams in Chinese), and you can also contact me with my English name Violet or Japanese name Yumeha (means wings of dreams in Japanese). I was born in midsummer and I am 22 this year. I studied business administration and language as an undergraduate in Tsinghua University in Beijing, China. ESG information disclosure is a passion of mine at that time, so for my dissertation I researched the effect of ESG disclosure on influences of companies’ green innovation accomplishments, and the impact on the market expectations. I’m now studying a Masters at London Business School because I want to broaden my understanding of business fundamentals, whilst deepening my specific interest in finance and investment.

I’ve had a variety of internships, from which I have been continually gaining competency and business skills in fields of investment banking, private equity and consulting during my university life. Because things I learnt from diverse backgrounds help me perform better when communicating with foreign clients and colleagues.
